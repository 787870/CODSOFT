## Task 2: Employee Performance Dashboard 👥

**Objective:** Design a comprehensive HR analytics dashboard to track employee performance, job satisfaction, and attrition demographics.

**Dataset Used:** IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition & Performance

**Key Features & DAX Measures:**
* **Executive KPIs:** Created custom DAX measures to calculate Total Employees (1,470), Average Performance Rating (3.15), and Total Attrition (237).
* **Performance Tracking:** Built a zoomed clustered column chart to compare average performance ratings across Research & Development, Human Resources, and Sales departments.
* **Satisfaction Analysis:** Visualized average job satisfaction across 9 distinct job roles using an ordered bar chart.
* **Demographic Insights:** Utilized an area chart to map total attrition against employee age, pinpointing specific age brackets with high turnover.
* **Interactive Filtering:** Implemented dropdown slicers for `Department` and `Gender` to allow for deep-dive, cross-filtered analytics.
